Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

This is a simple reciepe on how to make a grilled cheese sandwich with the proper ingredients! and the neccessary steps to make it!

Prep Time

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 5 Tbsp. unsalted butter, softened, divided
  • 4 slices sourdough bread
  • 2 c. shredded cheddar

Instructions

  1. Spread the butter: Spread 1 Tbsp. butter on one side of each slice of bread. Arrange buttered side down. Top 2 slices of bread with about 1 c. cheddar each. Top with another slice of bread buttered side up.
  2. Heat it Up: In a medium skillet over medium heat, melt 1 Tbsp. butter. Add 1 sandwich and cook until bread is golden brown and cheese is starting to melt, about 2 minutes. Flip sandwich and continue to cook until cheese is melty, about 30 seconds more. Transfer to a plate.
  3. Repeat: with the remaining sandwich, wiping out skillet if needed.
